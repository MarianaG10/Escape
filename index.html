Game Identity

A 3D labyrinth-style platformer focused on light exploration, accessible challenges, and friendly progression. The player advances by collecting keys and overcoming obstacles to escape from a dungeon or castle.

Design Pillars

1 — Friendly Exploration

The player is encouraged to explore without extreme pressure, discovering paths, secrets, and collectibles.

2 — Light and Progressive Challenge

Challenges gradually increase in difficulty, keeping the game engaging without becoming frustrating.

3 — Clear and Satisfying Progress

Each level has a clear objective (collect keys and unlock the exit), generating a constant sense of achievement.
