Game Identity

A 3D platformer labyrinth-style game focused on light exploration, accessible challenges, and friendly progression. The player advances by collecting keys and overcoming obstacles to escape a dungeon or castle, encouraging curiosity and steady accomplishment rather than punishment.

Design Pillars

1 — Friendly Exploration

The player is encouraged to explore without extreme pressure, discovering paths, secrets, and collectibles at their own pace.

2 — Light and Progressive Challenge

Challenges gradually increase in difficulty, keeping the experience engaging without becoming frustrating.

3 — Clear and Satisfying Progression

Each level has a clear objective (collect keys and open the exit), creating a constant sense of achievement.
